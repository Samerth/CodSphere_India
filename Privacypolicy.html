Effective Date: October 24, 2024

Last Updated: October 24, 2024

At Codsphere, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information depending on your interaction with our website.

  • Personal Information: We collect personal data when you provide it, such as name, email, phone number, and other information.
  • Non-Personal Information: Automatically collected data like IP address, browser type, pages visited, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support.

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share data with trusted third parties, legal authorities, and service providers as required.

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but cannot guarantee 100% security due to the nature of online transmissions.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices. Please review periodically for updates.
